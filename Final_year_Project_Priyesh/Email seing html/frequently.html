Frequently Asked Questions

1.How do I create an account on your platform?

You can create an account by clicking on the "Sign Up" or "Create Account" button on the homepage and filling out the required information such as your name, email address, and password.

2.What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards, and other secure payment gateways. You can view all available payment options during the checkout process.

3.How can I track my order

Once your order is confirmed and shipped, we will send you a tracking number via email. You can use this tracking number to track your order on our website or the shipping carrier's website.

4.What is your return policy?

Our return policy allows you to return eligible items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange. Please refer to our Returns & Refunds page for more details.

5.How do I contact customer support?
